About US

South East Queensland Scales

South East Queensland Scales has over 60 years of experience in the weighing industry.

We were established in Queensland back in 1993 and we've grown rapidly as a supplier of quality, reliable weighing equipment Australia wide.
We stock a wide range of weighing equipment and spare parts from Queensland. We are committed to giving our customers the best possible solutions to their weighing needs. Our after-sales service is second to none!

South East Queensland Scales currently hold the National Measurement Institute Service license # SL-0136 which enables us to verify a wide variety of weighing equipment either on-site or in our service facility.

Our History

1993 - Company established in South East Queensland
1994 - Commenced importing scales from Taiwan
1995 - Company appointed sole Australian distributorship of Haenni Wheel Load Scales, Switzerland
1999 - Company appointed sole Australian distributorship of Excell Precision Scales, Taiwan
2002 - Company appointed sole Australian distributorship of Jadever Scales, Taiwan
2006 - Company appointed sole Australian distributorship of Pris Scales, Taiwan
2005 - Company appointed sole Australasia distributorship of Haenni Wheel Load Scales, Switzerland
